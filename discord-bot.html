Clan Bot

A Discord bot developed in Python for my clan called Clan Bot in Naruto-Arena.net.

Naruto-Arena.net is a turn-based, tactical online multiplayer browser game played based on the Shounen Anime and Manga series called Naruto. You pick three characters to form a team and battle it out against other players. Players battle for different purposes: Ranking up to number 1, unlocking more characters, or having fun against friends by playing private battles. You can make friends by sending requests to players or through the naruto-arena discord server.

There is another aspect of the game called Clan Wars which are private battles played between clans, where the players go against each other under certain war rules

This bot was developed for the purpose of helping I and my clanmates in the Clan Wars by making it easier for us to build teams that meet the criteria of various war rules.

An example of a war rule given in Clan Wars is a banned list of characters whereby if a player uses any of the characters from that list, their opponent can claim a win against them from the match that the banned character was used.

Clan bot allows a user to type in any character's name as a discord message and once the user sends the message, a graphical representation of all characters EXCEPT the ones the user typed would be displayed. Hence the user was able to type out or copy and paste the banned list of characters, and Clan Bot provided all characters available for the user to legally use for his Clan War.

Clan Bot has successfully reduced the amount of times a player breaks a rule by using a banned character in a Clan War.
